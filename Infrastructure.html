Overview of Offering

 

 

 

 

Moving to the Microsoft Azure platform will mean a change in the skillsets, processes, and tools used within your organisation. Putting these changes in place often increases cost and takes focus away from core business challenges.

 

How can your organisation exploit the benefits offered by new technologies without increasing costs and disrupting business focus?

 

At Fujitsu we deliver exceptional IT Service and Operations Management. Building on this, we have invested our resources in applying our expertise not only to traditional infrastructures but also to new hybrid infrastructures that utilise the Azure platform. The service enables your organisation to adopt cloud computing, where extra resources can be added quickly to meet current demands, without the worry of managing the underlying complexity, processes and people. Fujitsu’s Cloud Service Azure enables organisations to shift investment from capital expense to operation expense.

 

Our Cloud Service Azure gives you ultimate flexibility and agility. The service is consumed in the same ‘pay as you use’ way, so you can benefit from a standard service, enabling you to keep pace with technological change without having to manage the complexities that go alongside it.

 

As well as providing managed service support, Fujitsu has an end-to-end portfolio of services to help your organisation ‘Digitilse with Confidence’. Supporting you with planning for the use of Azure within your organisation, we offer a highly standardised and automated services to integrate, automate and manage complex IT estates using a fast, secure and compliant approach, as well as co-creation services to drive innovation and transformational change within your organisation getting the most out of the new technologies that fit your organisation, at the volumes you require.

Cloud Service Azure Service Deliverables

Cloud Platform Deliverables

The Cloud Service Azure has been designed to enable as little or as much to be consumed depending on your requirements. To enable clear and transparent management options, each offering is split into 4 key areas:

·        Compute (Virtual Machines and Platform as a Service Offerings)

·        Networking and Tenancy Management

·        Storage

·        Database Management (Virtual Machine and Database as a Service Offerings)

 

Each area consists of 4 task categories that can be varied to suit operational and technology differences in the environments:

·        Manage - The typical management tasks that would be undertaken when managing the asset

·        Monitor – The ongoing monitoring tasks undertaken when managing the asset

·        Support – The support available for operational issues with the asset

·        Report – The reporting options available for the asset

 

Services provided to support the compliance, integration and operational effectiveness of your Cloud platform are focused around 4 main areas:

·        Security Management

·        Integration Management

·        Disaster Recovery

 

Service Options

Different workloads operating within the Azure environment will have different service requirements and therefore 2 levels of service are provided within our Cloud Service Azure to cater for the operational requirements of the different environments:

·        Standard – Fujitsu’s mid-tier service for customers who require assurances of the reliability and stability through monitoring and patching but within the business day

·        Enterprise – Fujitsu’s service designed for critical workloads offering 24-hour coverage so you have the confidence that your Azure platform will be managed and supported all day, every day.

Azure Compute

Managing Windows and Linux Virtual Machines (VMs).

 

Manage: Fujitsu will manage the provisioning of new VMs from existing managed images and ARM templates, or from the platform provider’s marketplace, as well as decommission workloads as required. We will apply configuration changes to the base operating system, apply auto-scaling rules and manage the patching process, applying patches at least monthly including testing and implementation of both system critical and security patches. We will also manage the backup and restore requirements for your workloads through a standard SLA and on an ad-hoc basis if required, using tagging strategies aligned to customers lifecycle management policies.

 

Monitor:  Ongoing monitoring of your Compute ensures a stable and reliable system enabling preventative and proactive action to avoid any disruption to the productivity of your business. Fujitsu will monitor the VMs in your Compute environment for items such as:

·        CPU Utilisation

·        Disk Performance

·        Network traffic in and out of your compute environment

·        Memory Utilisation and reservations in the Compute environment

·        Vendor escalated call performance

·        Demand Management for monitored Infrastructure Systems

·        Antivirus including weekly scanning of Virtual Machines

 

Support:  Investigation and resolution of incidents in scope logged against your chosen SLA. Proactive logging and resolution of incidents raised by the monitoring tools at defined thresholds.

 

Report: As a minimum Fujitsu will report on the following elements on a monthly basis:

·        Monitoring logs and thresholds breached

·        Incidents logged

·        SLA achievement

·        Billing and consumption

·        All change and service requests logged and actioned within the reporting period

 

Azure Network

Management and support for your Azure virtual networks, network gateways, VPN and ExpressRoute endpoints, ACLs, virtual network appliances and configuration. Across the platform supporting multiple network and VPN configurations. This includes the support of an ExpressRoute connection on the Azure platform, but not the provisioning of the ExpressRoute or any supporting network provider connections. A separate service from Fujitsu is available for providing a high speed, low-latency dedicated network, Hybrid Cloud Connect.

 

Manage: Fujitsu will manage your virtual network in Azure. Managing the Virtual Network itself including VNet, subnets, IP addresses, Access Control Lists, firewalls, load balancers and DNS settings, your S2S and P2P VPN connections (Azure side) anfdd the Azure side of your ExpressRoute connection and tenancy management across different zones. The service team will work with you to optimise fault tolerance and resilience across the Azure platform providing a stable and secure environment for your organisation and managing service requests for adapting the network structure whilst in service.

 

Monitor: Monitoring key thresholds and taking preventative action will enable your network to remain stable and reliable. We will monitor items on the Azure network such as:

·        Connection health for VPN and ExpressRoute connections (Azure side end point only)

·        Aggregate network inbound and outbound traffic

·        Expected network behaviour

·        Network configuration and change monitoring

·        Load balancing metrics and errors

 

Support: Investigation and resolution of incidents logged against your chosen SLA. Proactive logging and resolution of incidents raised by the monitoring tools.

 

Report: As a minimum will report on the following elements on a monthly basis:

·        Monitoring logs and thresholds breached

·        Incidents logged

·        SLA achievement

·        All change and service requests logged and actioned within the reporting period

·        Billing and consumption

 

Azure Storage

Management and support of your Azure storage environment for File, Disk, Blob, Data Lake, Archive, Backup and Archiving (snapshot) requirements for adherence to customer’s policies and compliance requirements.

For advanced backup and recovery features, including data deduplication,Cloud Service Azure integrates with Fujitsu’s Hybrid Data Protection service.

 

Manage: Fujitsu will manage your storage accounts and volumes within the Azure platform, carrying out requests such as the creation, configuration and deletion of your storage. Attaching and detaching of volumes, expansion of volumes and creation of volumes to/from snapshots using cloud native tooling. Fujitsu will configure access permissions and implement your data storage lifecycle and requests for recovery from your backup/archiving solution.

 

Monitor: Monitoring of your storage assets and identifying key thresholds will enable preventative action to be taken to minimise disruption, improve reliability and stability of your Azure environment. Fujitsu will monitor events and thresholds on your storage assets such as:

·        Capacity levels of your storage environment

·        The type and amount of storage consumed across different storage types

·        Backup health and recovery points

 

Support:  Investigation and resolution of incidents in scope logged against your chosen SLA. Logging and resolution of incidents raised proactively by the monitoring tools.

 

Report: As a minimum Fujitsu will report on the following elements on a monthly basis:

·        Monitoring Logs and thresholds breached

·        Incidents logged

·        SLA achievement

·        All change and service requests logged and actioned within the reporting period

·        Billing and consumption

 

Database Management

Management and support of your databases in your Azure platform. Fujitsu will manage your databases hosted on Azure Compute VM instance(s) or running on the Azure SQL service.

 

Manage: Fujitsu will manage your Database housekeeping and administration routines, the backup of your database to your defined backup solution, and manage and maintain scheduled tasks running on the instance. To ensure security of your data Fujitsu will manage User accounts, database roles and groups to your organisations security standards (as requested), applying database fixes and patches and minor updates (major updates will be treated as a separate project).

 

Monitor: Ongoing monitoring of the database services processes and environment, such as:

·        RDS CPU Utilisation

·        Disk read / write performance

·        Network Performance

·        Database Connections

·        Available Storage

 

Support: Incident management, workaround diagnostic and resolution including liaison with application provider, operating systems support teams and vendor support where required

 

Report: Fujitsu will report on service availability, processor memory and disk usage statistics and any potential impact to your database

Security Management

Identity and Access: Fujitsu will provide security management across your estate ensuring the confidence in the security of your platform and data. Managing Identity and Access Management across your platform including:

 

·        Creation of and management of users

·        Creation of and management of roles and policies

·        Creation of and management of groups

·        Adherence to your organisation’s identity and access management

·        Multi-Factor authentication

 

Operating Systems: Fujitsu will apply policies to the OS based upon your organisation’s current or future hardening policy. If a hardening policy needs to be created or updated Fujitsu can provide this service as an additional chargeable service

 

Security Monitoring: Fujitsu will configure Azure Security Center on your organisation’s compliance requirements with e-mail notifications. Fujitsu will manage and respond to security alerts and monitor e-mail notifications from ASC. All medium and high severity alerts will be escalated to the customer and logged as an incident for reporting purposes.
